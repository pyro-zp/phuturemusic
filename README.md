## About the Artist

**phuturemusic** is a Russian electronic music artist based in Moscow.  
Blending futuristic sound design with emotional melodies, his music explores the intersection between technology and human feeling.

Influenced by artists like **Kraftwerk**, **Daft Punk**, **Fatboy Slim**, and **Chemical Brothers**, he crafts immersive sonic experiences designed for both headphones and dancefloors.

Currently working on his next album *“[Virtuality]”*, expected in late 2026.
